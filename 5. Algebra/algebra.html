we notice that Δ ABO and Δ A'B'O are similar.

Similarly, Δ A'B'F and Δ OCF are similar, hence
$\hskip 1em \frac{A'B'}{OC}=\frac{FB'}{OF}$
But OC=AB
Hence,$\hskip 1em \frac{A'B'}{AB}=\frac{FB'}{OF}$ ..(2)
Equating eq(1) and (2), we get
$\hskip 1em \frac{OB'}{OB}=\frac{FB'}{OF}$
$\hskip 1em =\frac{OB'-OF}{OF}$
Substituting the sign convention, we get
OB = -u, OB' = v and OF = f
$\hskip 1em \frac{v}{-u}=\frac{v-f}{f}$
vf=-uv + uf
Or, uv=uf-vf
Dividing both the sides by uvf, we get
$\hskip 1em \frac{uv}{uvf}=\frac{uf}{uvf}-\frac{vf}{uvf}$
